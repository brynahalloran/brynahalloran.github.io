Staying Upright

The upside down is a tricky place, but there are a few telltale signs to look out for. We want your stay in Hawkins to be a pleasant one so please pay close attention. Staying rightside up is imperative to having a safe and enjoyable life. Here are some things to look out for;

Are you vomiting slugs? Is everything a dark, ominous color? Is a blood crazed monster trying to catch you? If these things are true then you may be in the upside down.


What to do

The number one thing to do is just not be there. If, however, you do end up in the upside down here is what to do. Remain calm, don't make a lot of noise, and hope that a girl with the numbers 011 finds you first.
